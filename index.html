AMM: An Automatic Mobile Manipulation System of Humanoid Robots for Industrial Applications

Author: Lisa Schroeder

Abstract

This paper presents a mobile manipulation system designed for humanoid robots, specifically applied in industrial environments. The system utilizes advanced algorithms and sensors to perform tasks autonomously, improving efficiency and safety in industrial operations...

Key Contributions

  • Development of a robust mobile manipulation system for humanoid robots.
  • Integration of advanced sensor fusion and real-time decision-making algorithms.
  • Implementation in industrial scenarios to enhance operational safety and performance.

Research and Results

The research explores key aspects of robot navigation, control, and manipulation in real-world industrial settings. The results demonstrate improvements in task completion time, robot maneuverability, and interaction with dynamic environments.
